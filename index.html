About

With the help of a VHS tutorial from the public library, I made his first web page in 6th grade. It had falling stars in the background, and all was right in the world. After completing coursework towards a degree in Marketing, I moved to New York City to pursue a career in fashion and lifestyle.

As fate might have it, I met a software engineer whose numerous conversations sparked a fire to pursue web developing as a career. Soon after, I enrolled in the Flatiron School, where I jump-started my career in web development. Since, I've being able to contribute code from starts-up all the way up to Fortune 200 companies with some amazing clients in between.

Having my code executed by millions of users, made my quest for well structured code an obsession and the pursuit of developing a more beautiful web, a fever.
